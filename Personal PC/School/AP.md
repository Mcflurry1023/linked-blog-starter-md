Ano ang sociological Theories?
Magbigay ng dalawang halimbawa at iexplain.

Sociological theories are assumptions meant to understand, inspect, and explain the socially constructed perspectives of the world, in an abstract and objective manner using a scientific methodology.

Feminist Theory; the way gender inequities and inequalities add on to social and political differences and how power is perpetuated between genders and societal gender roles.

Rational Choice Theory, models how individuals will behave and act based on expected utility and probability under uncertain situations.
